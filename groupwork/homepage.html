About ECHO

In a world where voices resonate and leave lasting impressions, ECHO embodies the diverse talents and ideas that define our collective. Much like an echo, we aim to amplify the powerful stories and perspectives that reverberate through our art, ensuring they resonate long after they are experienced. The Exhibition is the culmination of our three-year journey in the BFA in Digital Arts program at the University of Malta. Along this path, we have created projects that explore profound topics, including human rights, culture and heritage, mental health, technology, and the essence of creativity itself.

ECHO invites you to experience a symphony of digital art mediums, including editorial and graphic design, animation, photography, UI/UX design, mixed media, illustration, fashion design, VR, and video editing. Each piece echoes the individuality of its creator while harmonizing with the collective voice of the group. ECHO is an opportunity to delve into the boundless possibilities of digital art, where every idea resonates and every creation reflects the unique perspectives and innovative approaches of our students. Join us, and let the echoes of creativity inspire and connect us all.
